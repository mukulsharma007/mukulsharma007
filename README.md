## 🧠 About Me

Hey! I'm Mukul an AI/ML engineer who genuinely enjoys getting his hands dirty with models, pipelines, and data.
I've spent more than four years building NLP and ML systems in industry, and now I'm pushing that further with an MSc in Computer Science, going deep into Computer Vision, LLMs, and Generative AI.
Right now I'm building OCR and object detection systems for engineering systems, think YOLO models and document intelligence, not just notebooks, also i went a deep dive into RAG systems for question answering exploring their alterntives like reasoning augmentation, graph RAG and light RAG to build a state of art solution. 
I care about building things that actually work, not just things that look good in a demo. If that sounds like your kind of engineer, let's build. 🚀
